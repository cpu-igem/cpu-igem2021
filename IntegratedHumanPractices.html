  1. Garbage Disposal Transit Station in Jiangning High-tech Zone
    From 2009 to 2016, the state committed to outsourcing to promote the marketization process of public services. The municipal solid waste in Nanjing was outsourced by the government to qualified companies or social organizations to deal with. We visited several staff of waste treatment transfer station in Jiangning High-tech Zone to further investigate the processing process of PE, and they also gave us some suggestions for the project.

    a. The garbage in the city is mainly kitchen waste and other garbage, and PE plastic in waste packaging belongs to the scope of other garbage. The station can handle about 300 tons of other garbage a day.
    b. The main responsibility of the waste treatment transfer station in Jiangning High-tech Zone is to collect the waste collected at various locations within the scope of responsibility and then load it into large waste transport vehicles and transport it to distant waste disposal sites for incineration or landfill.
    c. The staff told us that the volume of other waste is huge, which requires manpower and material resources to be transported to incineration plant or landfill, and will cause indelible pollution to the environment.They argue that it would save resources and help the environment to a certain extent if the disposal of plastic waste could be narrowed down to individual households or communities.
    d. During the field investigation, we noticed that the garbage station had a serious odor. After talking with the staff, we learned that the odor also had a certain impact on their health. We reported this to our Collaboration team Tongji_CHINA 2021.We hope it will be helpful to their project.

  1. Nanjing Ecological Environment Bureau
    The ecological environment bureau is the functional organization of the local people's Government in charge of environmental protection. The marketization of public services does not mean the transfer of government responsibilities. The ecological environment bureau still has to assume the responsibility of supervision and management. We contacted the staff of Nanjing ecological environment bureau and consulted them on our project.

China pays more attention to the phenomenon of excessive packaging in the express industry, and has also issued relevant laws and regulations to limit it. The environmental bureau also supports and affirms our project. They suggested that we should pay attention to the practical significance of the project and consider cost-effectiveness. They said that we can try to find partners first, implement the technology into products through investment, and clarify the product positioning for subsequent promotion. As for technical issues, they recommended that we could seek help from relevant biotechnology companies and listen to the suggestions of professionals. At the same time, risk assessment and control shall be done well during the project.
